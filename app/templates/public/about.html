About Snip.space

Snip.space is an ad-free, free, and open source pastebin solution. We promise to keep it this way for as long as we are around.

Snip.space is available to anyone as an anonymous user, but grants additional functionality to users that have created and logged into their accounts.

Snip.space features include, 20+ languages of syntax highlighting, ability create/edit/delete snippets, downloading and retrieving raw snippet data.
